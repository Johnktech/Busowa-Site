New Four-Classroom Block: Construction Has Begun
We are thrilled to announce that groundwork for the new four-classroom block is officially underway. The construction, managed by local firm Shelter Solutions, will provide dedicated space for 280 additional learners in Primary 1–4. The project is expected to be completed within four months.

Tree Planting Drive: 200 Trees for the Future
Students, teachers, parents, and parish volunteers planted 200 trees across the school compound and surrounding land, as part of our environmental education program. The drive was led by Primary 5 and 6 students and supported by the local council.
