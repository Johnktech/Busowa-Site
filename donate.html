St. Antony Busowa Primary School was built for 350 pupils — 50 per classroom. Today it serves 1,000 children. In Primary 1 through Primary 4, each classroom holds 142 learners — nearly three times the recommended 55.

Teachers resort to splitting classes and teaching under trees. Individual attention is impossible. This project builds four dedicated classrooms using local materials and local builders — returning quality education to every child.

Construction Phases
1
Foundation & Brickwork ✓
Site cleared, foundation laid, brick walls rising. Local materials sourced. Municipal engineer on site.
2
Roofing & Windows
Roof installation, window frames, and ventilation openings to create well-lit, weather-proof learning spaces.
3
Plastering, Flooring & Furnishing
Interior finishing, painting, and fitting each classroom with desks, chairs, and blackboards.
4
Handover & Donor Report
Final inspection, official opening ceremony, and a full narrative, photographic, and financial report delivered to all donors.
